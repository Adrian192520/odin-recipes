mash platains recipe

Introduttion & ingredients: Mash platains are good and healthy latine amercan dish. ingredients :

  • platains
  • butter
  • water

cooking introduction

  1. peel the platains
  2. boil water in pan
  3. add the platains and boil cook
  4. drain the water and mash the platains
  5. add butter for taste
