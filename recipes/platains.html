Fry platains cook book

Introduttion: fry platains are latine american dish. Thier know for, how quicks it is to make and have good nutricianal value.

ingredients

  • cooking oil of choice
  • fresh plantains
  • salt and kepchup

introduction

  1. cut the platains
  2. in cooking pan pour oil and let it heat up to cooking tempature
  3. add the platains as is and allow it to cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  4. take out the uncook platains and using what ever tool of choice flatten the platains
  5. add the platain and fully cook them
  6. add as much salt and kepchup as like
